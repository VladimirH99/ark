Arkle Betting

The first and main Arkle utility is a horse racing betting site with a casino element and regular payouts to holders.

You chose number of the track the horse will ride and place your bet. New race will start every hour. Result is completely random. If any track is left without a bet, it will automatically be placed on our behalf.

If you choose a winning lane, bet amount is doubled, for 2-nd place you win 50% of your bet, for 3-rd place - 25%. For 4th-8th place you will lose the bet.

All proceeds from platform activities will be distributed to the Arkle Foundation and holders. Payments will be on a monthly basis.
